My Hobbies

One of my all-time favorite hobbies is learning new things... the list of which is ever-changing! Right now on tap for learning is:

  1. The Adobe Suite (currently on Adobe Indesign)
  2. Web Development/Coding through this awesome course
  3. Spanish Lessons through Duolingo
  4. American Sign Language courses on Udemy

Another way I spend free time is reading, or listening to audiobooks... I'm on my 27th book since the start of 2023! Here are the top books I've read this year, in my opinion!

  1. Shadow Divers by Robert Kurson - Non-fiction, an immersion into the world of deep sea wreck diving.. fascinating..thrilling!
  2. Fairy Tale by Stephen King - Most Stephen King books are too scary for me, but this is one of his ventures into the fantasy genre it is a really great read
  3. The Seven Husbands of Evelyn Hugo by Taylor Jenkins Reid - I listened to this one on Audible.. fantastic narration! Most of this historcial fiction novgel was set in the 1950's and the author does an excellent job setting the scene
  4. A Walk in the Woods: Rediscovering American on the Appalachian Trail by Bill Bryson - This read was SO interesting.. and at times, harrowing! Reading this has inspired me to do a "through hike" on one of the nation's famous hiking trails.. it's now officially on my bucket list!

I'm also really into self-care.. and something that I consider both a joy and a priority is exercise! My favorite forms of exercise are:

  • Walking - Walking alone, or with my Mom, I'm really into long walks and they always leave me feeling better than when I started. I need these for my mental health more than anything!
  • Yoga - My go-to is Yoga with Adriene on Youtube. She's such a calming sweet presence, has endless free resources (check out the many themed 30-day yoga challenges she offers), and even makes you laugh. A fabulous pick-me up and like a big hug to yourself!
  • Weights and Interval Training - Youtube Trainer Sydney Cummings Houdyshell gives has amazing variety of workout videos with a punch of positivity for all needs, and even if you arent looking to sweat, her 10, 15, 30 minute stretch videos are awesome.
